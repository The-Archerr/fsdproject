CollegeConnect

About CollegeConnect

CollegeConnect is a student-focused web application designed to streamline your academic journey. Built by four passionate students—Usha Sree Poola, Dishoja K, Selvi Sohitha T, and Reddy Usha—from Annamacharya Institute of Technology and Sciences (AITS) for a Full Stack Development (FSD) hackathon, this project aims to connect students with essential college resources in an intuitive and engaging way.

Features

  1. Syllabus: Access detailed course syllabi for subjects like Managerial Economics, Operating Systems, and more. Navigate through units with an interactive, collapsible layout to focus on what matters.
  2. Calendar: Stay updated with college events, deadlines, and exams. Our calendar helps you plan your semester effectively.
  3. Productivity: Boost your efficiency with tools like a task manager to track assignments and a Pomodoro timer for focused study sessions.
  4. AI Quiz: Test your knowledge with AI-generated quizzes tailored to your courses, making learning fun and interactive.
  5. Contact Us: Have questions or feedback? Reach out to our team directly via email for support.

How to Use CollegeConnect

Getting started is simple:

  1. Explore Features: Click the buttons below to access the Syllabus, Calendar, Productivity tools, AI Quiz, or Contact Us pages.
  2. Syllabus: Browse courses and expand units to view detailed topics. Use it to prepare for classes or review material.
  3. Calendar: Check upcoming events and add them to your schedule. Filter by date or type to stay organized.
  4. Productivity: Add tasks to the task manager and set Pomodoro timers (25-minute work sessions with 5-minute breaks) to stay on track.
  5. AI Quiz: Select a subject, answer questions, and get instant feedback to gauge your understanding.
  6. Contact Us: Email us with any issues or suggestions. We’re here to help!

CollegeConnect is designed to be user-friendly and responsive, working seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices. Whether you’re planning your studies or testing your skills, we’ve got you covered.
